Get help with “Manage Themes”

Themes give your WordPress style. Once a theme is installed, you may preview it, activate it or deactivate it here.

You can find additional themes for your site by using the new Theme Browser/Installer functionality or by browsing the WordPress Theme Directory directly and installing manually. To install a theme manually, upload its ZIP archive with the new uploader or copy its folder via FTP into your wp-content/themes directory.

Once a theme is uploaded, you should see it on this page.
